About Us

The modern world of agriculture sees the use of artificial intelligence, blockchain, machine learning, satellite imagery, computer vision, drones, advanced sensors, automation, and so many aspects that were unheard of a few decades ago. Team 'Sichai' focus on those technologies that will help famers reduce their workload rather replacing them with machineries. Our team focus on beautifying the traditional culture by adding resources and help.
